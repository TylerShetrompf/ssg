Chicken Fun Facts!

* Turns out, chickens are pretty smart critters. They can solve problems, know who they are, and even remember stuff. Pretty wild, huh? * A bunch of chickens is called a "flock." Baby chickens are "chicks," the girls are "hens," and the boys are "roosters." * Chickens eat all sorts of things: seeds, bugs, even tiny lizards. They're like little garden helpers! * They've also got this whole social thing going on with a "pecking order" that shows who's boss. Drama, drama!
